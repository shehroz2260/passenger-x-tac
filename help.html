Passenger-X Help Center

Welcome to the Passenger-X Help Center. Here you will find answers to common questions about using the app.

1. Getting Started

Passenger-X uses Email Verification for account creation. No OTP is required.

  • Sign up using your email address
  • Verify your email via the link sent to your inbox
  • Login after successful verification

2. Passenger & Driver Modes

Passenger-X has two modes:

  • Passenger Mode: Request rides from nearby drivers
  • Driver Mode: Receive and accept ride requests

You can switch modes from your profile if available.

3. Finding Nearby Drivers

When a passenger opens the app, Passenger-X automatically shows all available drivers within a 10 km radius.

  • Passenger can view nearby drivers
  • Passenger can send a ride request to any driver
  • Driver can accept or reject the request

4. Ride Requests

Passengers can send ride requests to drivers of their choice. Drivers receive notifications and may accept or decline requests.

5. Profile & Account Settings

From the user profile, you can:

  • Edit account details
  • Update personal information
  • Delete your account permanently

Account deletion is irreversible.

6. Subscription

Passenger-X offers subscription plans that unlock additional features or benefits.

  • Subscriptions are managed through the app
  • Charges are billed periodically
  • You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account

7. Safety & Responsibility

Drivers and passengers are expected to behave respectfully and follow all applicable laws while using the platform.
